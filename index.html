    About Us

    E-Actuell, a partnership firm is committed to bringing new and novel technological products to the global market through design and development in-house. The products that are designed and developed are assessed for engineering excellence and adhere to International standards.
    Our team is equipped with well-experienced engineers who can design, develop, test and validate the product whilst complying with all statutory, functional, performance and customer requirements.

    Our Vision

    To enable economic growth by adding values, setting product standards to promote re-usability for the betterment of society and the planet’s future.

    Our Mission

    • Selecting the products having the potential to bring down carbon footprints and landfills by providing a high degree of recyclability wherever possible and bringdown the life cycle cost.
    • To produce the long-lasting products by taking every bit of opportunity through research and development.
    • To discover newer ways of making things by promoting ‘out of box’ thinking.
