Research and Development

  • Phase 1 Innovation proposal – Client’s aspiration for a new or localized product
    • Innovation proposal refers to the introduction of new ideas that will replace existing products / features intending to improve or disrupt existing applications or creating new solutions. The need for innovation is usually trigged by the market promotion team who gathers market needs with well a defined scope.
  • Phase 2 Innovation Offer – Overview of product
    • In this phase, formulating requirements, capture abstract ideas into a detailed requirements document and a product feasibility study to overview how the product looks like with the estimated cost. The list of contractual documents as below are agreed upon in this phase:
    • Standard scope:
    • Design - 2d drawings and 3d pictures including interface controls.
    • Proof of concept - Development of in-house prototype using 3d printing and other machined structures.
    • Optional scope (other contractual documents):
    • Design description document.
    • Finite element simulation report.
    • Manufacturing strategy document.
    • Design for assembly document.
    • Calculation of intended “mean time between failure - MTBF” and “mean time to repair MTTR”.
    • Product life cycle costing covering preventive maintenance schedules.
    • Maintenance manual.
    • Technical information manual.
    • Test plan, test spec and validation report.
  • Phase 3 Design of concepts – An outlook of how the product is going to be.
    • The project moves on generating several workable concepts. The concepts are then scrutinized in a design review process using a check list and presentation template with questionaries covering interfaces, Performance, FMEA, DfA, DfM, RAMS, LCC, Service pedigree and validation arguments concluding a design freeze.
  • Phase 4 Proof of delivery met – A Working prototype model tested.
    • This is the phase where the concept take shape into a working prototype model. All the functionality and performance are evaluated and optimized for the final design.
  • Phase 5 Detailed design – Documents for manufacturing and realization.
    • Detailing of design along with the agreed scope of deliverables as specified in Phase 2 will be generated.
